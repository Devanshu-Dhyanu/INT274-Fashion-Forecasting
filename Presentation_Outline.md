### Slide 2: The Problem (The Waste Crisis)
- Fashion industry = 10% of global carbon emissions.
- Overproduction leads to billions of dollars in unsold stock.
- Lack of data-driven forecasting in niche ethical boutiques.

### Slide 3: Our Solution
- **Objective:** Build a "Conscious Demand Forecaster."
- **Approach:** Use ML to predict demand precisely to minimize inventory waste.
- **Goal:** Balance sustainability with business profitability.

### Slide 4: System Architecture
- **Data Input:** Store profiles + Historical sales.
- **Core Engine:** XGBoost (Ensemble Learning) + PCA (Dimensionality Reduction).
- **Deployment:** Real-time interactive dashboard on Streamlit Cloud.

### Slide 5: Store Profiling (K-Means & PCA)
- **Clustering:** How we group stores (Flagships vs. Small Boutiques).
- **Visualization:** Showing the PCA projection of store "identities."
- **Insight:** Understanding regional and categorical demand patterns.

### Slide 6: The Forecaster (XGBoost Engine)
- Why XGBoost? Handles non-linear trends and complex features (Promos, Seasons).
- Feature Importance: Showing how Eco-Ratings and Promos drive demand.
